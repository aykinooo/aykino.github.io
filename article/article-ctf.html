HTB Writeup: What Is Hack The Box & CTFs?

Feb 1, 2026

Capture The Flag platforms like Hack The Box are practical environments to learn offensive and defensive security. They provide legal, structured challenges that improve methodology and technical depth.

What Is a CTF?

A CTF challenge usually asks you to chain multiple small weaknesses to retrieve a flag. Common categories include web, binary exploitation, cryptography, reverse engineering, and forensics.

How to Approach HTB

Start with reconnaissance, collect evidence, test assumptions, and document each step. A repeatable workflow is more important than isolated tricks.

Skills You Build

CTFs improve technical writing, scripting, debugging, and problem decomposition. They also help you learn tooling such as Burp Suite, nmap, Wireshark, Ghidra, and pwntools.

Takeaway

If you are beginning in cybersecurity, CTFs are one of the fastest ways to gain hands-on experience and build a public portfolio through writeups.
